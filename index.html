About Me

Hi there! My name is Barrett. I'm a 2017 graduate of the University of Kentucky with a Bachelor’s of Science degree in Integrated Strategic Communication and a minor in Business. I completed my undergraduate studies summa cum laude and immediately headed south to the Peach State to begin my career as the Marketing and Communications Manager for the Atlanta-based IT staffing agency, eHire.

Most recently, I’ve taken my talents into the world of real estate as a full-time Realtor with Keller Williams Atlanta Partners. While I’ve enjoyed the thrill and excitement of this industry, I’m eager to shift my focus into the software development arena where opportunity for growth and innovation is boundless. I’m working to perfect my craft through Georgia Tech’s immersive coding boot camp with hopes that I’ll be able to take my newfound knowledge and experiences and transform them into a thriving, high-impact career as a software developer. Onward!
